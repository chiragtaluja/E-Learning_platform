About SkillSwap

Welcome to SkillSwap – A Room-Based Knowledge Sharing Platform!

SkillSwap is a space where learners and experts come together to exchange knowledge, ideas, and skills in organized virtual rooms.

What is SkillSwap?

SkillSwap allows users to create or join topic-based discussion rooms where they can ask questions, share experiences, and learn from others. It’s designed to foster meaningful learning communities driven by real-time engagement and mutual support.

Why I Built SkillSwap

As a self-taught developer and lifelong learner, I often felt the need for a more collaborative learning space. That’s what inspired me to build SkillSwap – a platform to make knowledge-sharing more interactive, accessible, and community-driven.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To connect curious minds and passionate mentors in real-time rooms where learning thrives through sharing.

Vision: To create a global community where knowledge flows freely, and every skill has a space to grow.

About the Developer

I'm Chirag, a BCA student and tech enthusiast passionate about web development, problem-solving, and building tools that empower learning. SkillSwap is a personal project that reflects my commitment to collaborative education and open knowledge.

What’s Next

SkillSwap is just getting started. I plan to add features like private rooms, scheduling, expert tagging, and gamified learning paths. Feedback and collaboration are always welcome!
